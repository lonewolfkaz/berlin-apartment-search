## How to Read This Document

| Field | Explanation |
|-------|-------------|
| **€/sqm** | Approximate price per square meter on resale or remaining primary stock |
| **75 sqm all-in** | Estimated total cost (Kaufpreis + Nebenkosten) for a 75 sqm unit. Without broker = ×1.08, with broker = ×1.1157 |
| **HBF** | Travel time to Berlin Hauptbahnhof by public transit |
| **Energy** | Energieeffizienzklasse per Energieausweis (A+ best, H worst) |
| **Status** | 🟢 on sale · 🟡 possibly available · ⚪ monitor · 🔴 nothing available · ❌ over budget |
